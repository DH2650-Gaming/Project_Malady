A Dream of a Little Hero

The magical bedtime world of a boy named Bryan

It was all just a dream—a dream of a little boy named Bryan.

As night gently fell, Bryan drifted into sleep and entered a world of his own making. His familiar bedroom transformed into a land of wonder and adventure. Towers built from blocks, castle walls made of pillows, and gates guarded by stuffed animals—all the everyday things around him were suddenly filled with magic.

Adorable, odd little creatures crept out from the corners, not so much enemies as mischievous visitors. But in his dream, Bryan was their brave protector. Wrapped in a blanket like a cape, dressed in his cozy pajamas, his hair still tousled from sleep—he was a little hero, as if returned from a journey to a kingdom among the stars.

In this dream world, he smiled, ran, and imagined freely—until the morning light peeked through the curtains and gently called him back.

But the dream never truly ends. Because as long as he closes his eyes, the story begins again.
